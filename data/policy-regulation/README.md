# Policy and Regulation Data

Publicly available policy and regulatory documentation affecting CEA operations, compared across state and tribal jurisdictions.

## Subfolders
- state-policy/ - state-level policy documentation
- tribal-policy/ - tribal-level policy documentation
- water-rights/ - water rights documentation by jurisdiction
- zoning-taxation/ - zoning rules and tax incentive documentation

## Status
Early stage. Full comparative analysis of state vs tribal policy is a longer-term research deliverable.
